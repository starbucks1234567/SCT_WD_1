Major AI News Updates (Late December 2025)





Top Industry Moves & Acquisitions


  • Meta Acquires Manus:
    Meta has agreed to acquire Singapore-based AI startup Manus for over $2 billion, strengthening its deep research and autonomous task capabilities.

  • Brookfield’s $10B AI Fund:
    Brookfield Asset Management launched a cloud business to lease high-end AI chips, backed by a $10 billion fund.

  • IBM Strategic Acquisitions:
    IBM acquired Confluent to build a smart data platform for enterprise generative AI and partnered with Riyadh Air to launch the world’s first AI-native airline.


Corporate Strategy & Scaling


  • Agentic AI Focus:
    Industry leaders, including Andrew Ng, emphasize Agentic AI—systems capable of autonomous multi-step actions—as the next major commercial value driver.

  • Infrastructure Surge:

    • Microsoft committed $23 billion to AI infrastructure in India and Canada.

    • Oracle reported 68% growth in cloud infrastructure revenue, though AI-related capital expenditures are pressuring margins.

    • China’s AI chip firm Biren raised $717 million via a Hong Kong IPO to compete in the specialized AI hardware market.


Regulation & Ethics


  • UN Resolution on Nuclear AI:
    The United Nations adopted its first resolution addressing AI risks in nuclear weapon systems.

  • Job Displacement Concerns:
    The Bank of England and DeepSeek researchers renewed warnings regarding AI’s potential to displace jobs as systems become more autonomous.


Emerging Tech & Product Updates


  • Meta SAM 3 & SAM 3D:
    Meta introduced Segment Anything Model 3 (SAM 3) and SAM 3D, enabling advanced 3D reconstruction and human body estimation for computer vision.

  • Google AI Recap:
    Google closed 2025 with over 60 major announcements across Gemini, Search, Pixel, and AI-powered scientific research tools.
